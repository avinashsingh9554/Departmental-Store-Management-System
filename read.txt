1.) This website is a Departmental Store Management System website which helps users to maintain and keep track of all necessary 
records of their shop. 

2.) Features of the Website:
2.1 It can store all the categories of products available in shop
2.2 Owner of the shop can add more categories as if it is available in the shop
2.3 Website can store the details of all kinds of products available in the shop
2.4 Website can keep the record of customers as which customer purchased           which product and on what date
2.5 It is also provides the seraching facility such as searching a particular product
2.6 Shows information and description of products and categories 
2.7 Robust database backend and easy to update information
